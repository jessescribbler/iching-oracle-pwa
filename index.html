    Sources and approach

    About this oracle

    The I Ching, or Book of Changes, is an ancient Chinese work used for reflection on change, timing, and conduct.

    This app uses the traditional King Wen sequence, hexagram structure, and three-coin casting method. Its short reflections are original app text rather than a claim to replace a full translation.

    Public-domain foundation

    For deeper reading, consult James Legge’s 1882 English translation and the original Chinese text. Both are public domain in the United States.

    Project Gutenberg: Chinese Yi Jing James Legge scan: Sacred Books of the East

    Treat a reading as a perspective to consider. For health, legal, financial, or safety decisions, seek qualified advice and use your own judgment.
