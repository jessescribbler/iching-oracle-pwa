    About Recovery Cottage

    The I Ching, or Book of Changes, is an ancient Chinese work used for reflection on change, timing, relationship, and conduct.

    Recovery Cottage is an original interpretive rendering inspired by the traditional King Wen sequence, the ancient Chinese text, and James Legge’s public-domain translation. It is not presented as a philological or official translation.

    The Recovery Cottage reflections bring the oracle into conversation with recovery, grief, boundaries, accountability, patience, repair, and the slow work of becoming more whole.

    Public-domain foundation

    James Legge’s 1882 English translation and the original Chinese text are public domain in the United States. Modern translations and commentary remain the work of their authors and are not copied here.
